About Me

I am Basil John Milton Muthuraj, an MSBA graduate with a deep-rooted passion for Data Science, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence. My professional journey began as an ML Data Associate at Amazon, where I developed a strong interest in the potential of data to drive innovation.

Motivated to do more, I pursued a Data Science Engineering course to strengthen my foundational knowledge and dive deeper into advanced concepts. To broaden my expertise further, I enrolled in the Master's in Business Analytics program, where I learned to bridge the gap between data science techniques and their applications in the business world.

As part of my capstone project, I built an AI Agent designed to streamline data workflows, which sparked my curiosity about the transformative potential of Generative AI. Since then, I have been closely following the daily advancements in the field of Gen AI and its impact on reshaping industries, especially the field of Data Science.

I am committed to leveraging my skills and insights to solve complex data-driven problems and create impactful, real-world solutions that make a difference.
